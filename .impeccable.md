## Design Context

### Users
Primary design target: home-health agency administrators who often wear multiple hats across operations, clinical oversight, and executive-style admin coordination. They use the Admin Dashboard during the workday to monitor approvals, scheduling, messaging, compliance, records, and organizational health from one surface.

### Brand Personality
The interface should feel like a high-tech mission control environment for care operations: precise, capable, responsive, and operationally serious. It should create moments of cinematic polish without becoming playful, game-like, or flashy for its own sake.

### Aesthetic Direction
Direction: cinematic mission control for healthcare operations.

Visual intent:
- advanced and premium rather than sterile
- dense and informed rather than generic SaaS
- polished motion and transitions rather than decorative noise

Anti-references:
- avoid gaming UI / cyberpunk theatrics
- avoid bland generic SaaS dashboard patterns

### Design Principles
1. Treat the Admin Dashboard as a command surface: fast situational awareness first, spectacle second.
2. Use motion to connect operational state changes, especially navigation, review actions, and panel transitions.
3. Favor cinematic restraint over visual noise; every extraordinary effect must support comprehension.
4. Keep the experience credible for healthcare operations: trustworthy, sharp, and efficient.
5. Differentiate from generic dashboards through choreography, hierarchy, and system-level polish rather than gimmicks.
